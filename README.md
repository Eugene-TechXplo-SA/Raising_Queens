# Raising-Queens-Website
It is a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) platform dedicated to supporting women and children through awareness, community engagement, and online donations. The platform serves as a central space where individuals can learn about the organization’s mission, understand ongoing initiatives, and contribute financially to make a positive impact.

Our mission is to empower women and protect children by providing access to resources, support systems, and opportunities that improve their quality of life. We believe that every woman and child deserves safety, dignity, education, and hope for a better future.

What the Website Does
-Provides information about the organization, its values, and goals
-Educates visitors about issues affecting women and children
-Allows users to make donations online
-Encourages community involvement and awareness
-Builds transparency and trust with supporters
